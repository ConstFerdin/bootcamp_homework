Recipes

Enjoy the aromatic and flavorful Chicken Biryani with this easy-to-follow recipe. Perfect for special occasions or a delightful family meal.

How to Prepare?

How to? Follow these simple steps to make Chicken Biryani:

  1. Marinate the chicken with spices and yogurt.
  2. Cook the basmati rice separately.
  3. Layer the marinated chicken and partially cooked rice in a pot.
  4. Garnish with fried onions, mint, and cilantro.
  5. Allow the biryani to cook on low heat until the flavors blend perfectly.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy the delicious Chicken Biryani!

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 500g
  • Basmati Rice: 2 cups
  • Onions: 2, thinly sliced
  • Tomatoes: 2, chopped
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tablespoon
  • Biryani Masala: 2 tablespoons
  • Bay Leaves: 2
  • Cardamom: 4 pods
  • Cloves: 4
  • Cinnamon Stick: 1
  • Mint Leaves: A handful
  • Cilantro: A handful
  • Ghee: 2 tablespoons
  • Oil: 3 tablespoons
  • Salt: To taste
