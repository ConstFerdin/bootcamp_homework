Manapad Escapade

Discover the Charm of Manapad

Welcome to my travel blog as I unravel the beauty and culture of Manapad, a serene coastal village nestled in Tamil Nadu, India. Join me on an unforgettable journey filled with sun-soaked beaches, rich history, and warm hospitality.

Top Places to Visit

  • Manapad Beach

    Enjoy the tranquility of the pristine Manapad Beach with its golden sands and clear blue waters. Perfect for a leisurely stroll or a relaxing day by the sea.

    • Sunbathe on the sandy shores
    • Explore nearby fishing villages
    • Try local seafood delicacies

  • Manapad Church

    Visit the historic Holy Cross Church, known for its Portuguese influence and the annual 'Santhome Festival.' Marvel at the beautiful architecture and serene surroundings.

    • Attend the Santhome Festival
    • Learn about the church's history
    • Enjoy panoramic views from the hill

Things to Do

  1. Take a boat ride to explore the coastline
  2. Visit the local fish market and witness the daily catch
  3. Attend a cultural event or festival if timing allows
